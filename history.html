History of the Krusty Krab

The Krusty Krab was founded by none other than Eugene H. Krabs, better known as Mr. Krabs. After a distinguished career in the Navy, Mr. Krabs invested his life's savings in building a small but mighty fast-food empire. The restaurant is famous across Bikini Bottom for its delicious Krabby Patties.

The Krusty Krab was built on the remains of a failed retirement home called the "Rusty Krab." With Mr. Krabs' dedication and savvy business skills, the restaurant flourished. It's been the main source of income (and joy) for Mr. Krabs ever since.
