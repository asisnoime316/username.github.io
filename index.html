Home of the World-Famous Krabby Patty

At the Krusty Krab, we serve up delicious, freshly made Krabby Patties every day. Whether you're a sponge, a starfish, or a land-dwelling squirrel, our patties are sure to satisfy your cravings!

The Krusty Krab is Bikini Bottom’s most famous restaurant, known for serving the legendary Krabby Patty! Owned by the penny-pinching Mr. Krabs, this humble diner is where SpongeBob works tirelessly as a fry cook to ensure that every Krabby Patty is cooked to perfection. It’s not just a restaurant; it’s a piece of Bikini Bottom’s culture and the source of many underwater hijinks! Come by for a bite, and taste the magic that keeps customers coming back for more.
