Meet the Staff

Mr. Krabs

Role: Owner and Manager

Mr. Eugene Krabs, a shrewd businessman with an insatiable love for money, is the founder and owner of the Krusty Krab. He’ll do anything to protect his secret Krabby Patty formula.

SpongeBob SquarePants

Role: Fry Cook

SpongeBob is the energetic and optimistic fry cook of the Krusty Krab. He takes immense pride in making the perfect Krabby Patty and is always eager to please the customers.

Squidward Tentacles

Role: Cashier

Squidward, the grumpy cashier of the Krusty Krab, prefers to spend his time playing the clarinet and avoiding work. He tolerates SpongeBob’s enthusiasm but isn’t too fond of the job.
