The Krusty Krab's Famous Customers

SpongeBob SquarePants

Although he works at the Krusty Krab, SpongeBob occasionally sneaks a bite of the Krabby Patty, his favorite food.

Patrick Star

Patrick loves Krabby Patties more than anything else and eats as many as he can get his hands on.

Squidward Tentacles

Squidward initially pretended to hate Krabby Patties, but later discovered he secretly loves them (to an unhealthy degree).

Mr. Krabs

The owner of the Krusty Krab loves his profits more than the food, but he’s had his fair share of Krabby Patties.

Plankton

Plankton finally tasted a Krabby Patty after many attempts to steal the formula, though it wasn’t part of his original plan.

Sandy Cheeks

As a squirrel from Texas, Sandy enjoys the Krusty Krab after a long day of science and karate. Her favorite dish: a spicy Krabby Patty.

Larry the Lobster

The Krusty Krab is Larry’s go-to place for a post-workout meal. He orders double Krabby Patties to fuel his muscles.

Mrs. Puff

After dealing with SpongeBob’s driving, Mrs. Puff unwinds with a Krabby Patty. It helps calm her nerves!

Mermaid Man

Even superheroes need to eat! Mermaid Man is a regular at the Krusty Krab, where he enjoys the classic Krabby Patty combo.

Barnacle Boy

Barnacle Boy prefers a simpler meal, usually a Krabby Patty with no pickles. He's always complaining about something!

Bubble Bass

Bubble Bass is notorious for being picky with his Krabby Patties. He often tries to get free food by claiming there are no pickles.

Man Ray

The infamous villain Man Ray tasted a Krabby Patty during his reformation attempts. It's hard to tell if he actually liked it.

The Flying Dutchman

The Flying Dutchman, despite being a ghost, once came down to the Krusty Krab to have a Krabby Patty as part of his haunting routine.

Perch Perkins

Bikini Bottom’s famous news anchor has mentioned Krabby Patties on-air and has eaten at the Krusty Krab on special occasions.

Old Man Jenkins

Old Man Jenkins has been a long-time patron of the Krusty Krab, having witnessed the rise of the Krabby Patty’s popularity over the years.

Nat Peterson

Nat is one of the Krusty Krab’s most loyal customers, often seen enjoying Krabby Patties with great enthusiasm.
