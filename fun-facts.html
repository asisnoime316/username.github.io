Fun Facts About the Krusty Krab

1. The Krabby Patty Secret Formula

The secret formula for the Krabby Patty is one of the most closely guarded secrets in Bikini Bottom!

2. The First Krabby Patty

The first Krabby Patty was created by Mr. Krabs in the early days of the Krusty Krab, back in 1999!

3. SpongeBob's Dream Job

SpongeBob has always dreamed of being a fry cook, and he got his wish when he joined the Krusty Krab!

4. The Krusty Krab's Rival

The Krusty Krab's main competitor is the Chum Bucket, owned by Plankton, who is always trying to steal the secret formula!

5. Krabby Patty Consumption

In the series, the Krusty Krab sells more than a million Krabby Patties every day!
